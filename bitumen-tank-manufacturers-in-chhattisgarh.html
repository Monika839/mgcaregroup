Bitumen Tank Manufacturer in Chhattisgarh

Maa Gayatri Construction & Road Equipments bitumen storage tanks in Chhattisgarh are designed for storing liquid asphalt cement (Bitumen) and Polymer Modified Asphalt. They are used mainly at hot mix asphalt plants. They have evolved to a high level of perfection over the many years we have made them.

Our factory-built bitumen tanks are available in a wide variety of sizes in Chhattisgarh. Two inches of fiberglass insulation is our standard for tanks used to store asphalt cement. Tanks are manufactured in cylindrical or U-shape. All tanks are thoroughly insulated all around.
Bitumen is pumped to the dryer drum through pipe lines. Bitumen Tanks in Chhattisgarh are fitted with independent on-off type burners. These asphalt tanks are most reliable and energy efficient units. These tanks are fully automatic and take low operating costs. Moreover there is no coking or hot spots apart from these the tanks recover from temperature rapidly.

Customers can opt for a direct heating type or hot oil heated bitumen tanks. The indirect heating tanks are supplied with an optional thermal oil heater. The Direct heating tanks are supplied with automatic imported pressure jet burner. As a standard all tanks are supplied with auto thermostatic controls, hot oil circulating systems and jacketed bitumen pump. Maa Gayatri Construction & Road Equipments tanks are one of the best asphalt bitumen tanks available in Chhattisgarh.
