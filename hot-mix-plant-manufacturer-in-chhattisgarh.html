What are Asphalt Hot Mix Plants?

Asphalt Hot Mix Plant is a single plant that is used to mix dry warm aggregate with asphalt to produce a homogeneous mix at the proper temperature. It is often commonly used in the construction of roadways, streets, highways, parking lots, and other things.
